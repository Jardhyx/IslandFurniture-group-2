Services

Delivery
"You can take it home yourself, but you don't have to !"

Flat-packed products are easy and efficient for you to transport. But you can also arrange delivery straight to your home or your business. If you prefer to have your purchase delivered, we have a wide array of delivery options to pick from when shopping at Island Furniture. Our delivery services are designed to be flexible and convenient, ensuring that your items arrive safely and on time.

Assembly
"You can build it yourself, but you don't have to !"

Our assembly fee is 20% of the original product retail price for all other furniture that requires assembly, and 10% of the original product retail price for sofa and sofa bed items. This service is only available for truck deliveries and also includes the installation of tip-over restraints for safety purposes. However, the service does not include electrical and plumbing.

Planning Guides & Tools
"You can plan it yourself, but you don't have to !"

With our planning tools, take your first step to becoming your own designer! Browse our many solutions and customize your sofa, wardrobes, workspaces, or even the bathroom. Please note that some planning tools may take some time to be delivered in the process. Approach any of our co-workers at your Island Furniture store near you to help you with any home furnishing or design needs you have.
